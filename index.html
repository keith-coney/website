“Going from Suicide to Superman”

Helping people overcome adversity.

Keith Coney is a Chartered Engineer and Chartered Project Manager, an Accredited Life Coach (Being Profile), Mental Health First Aider and an Accredited Lead Auditor. He works full time but in his spare time he resolves any Psychiatric Mental Illnesses and any Emotional Traumas, as well as people with multiple disorders. Keith does not work with Personality Disorders.

Keith has no training in mental health with the exception of a mental health first aid course and a great deal of personal experience of his own and with counselling others. Keith has experienced 4 mental illnesses and recovered more than a decade ago and has gone from strength to strength since. Despite previously having a strong fear of public speaking, Keith has grown to embrace his experiences and has now spoken to many large businesses, charities and International Conferences about his innovative work in mental health.

Keith is an Empath and as a result he has an unusual way of working and it is important that you keep an open mind in order to achieve exceptional results.

Keith is an advocate for the Eradication of Mental Illnesses. Follow Keith on LinkedIn.
